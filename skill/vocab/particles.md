## 常见易混对比组

### 位置距离谱 (近→远)
NEXT TO > BY > BESIDE > NEAR > AT

### 上/下组
- UP / OVER / ABOVE / ON TOP OF (上面)
- DOWN / UNDER / BELOW / UNDERNEATH / BENEATH (下面)

### 里/外组
- IN / INSIDE / WITHIN (里面)
- OUT / OUTSIDE / BEYOND (外面)

### 穿过组
- THROUGH (内部穿过，如隧道)
- ACROSS (表面穿过，如马路)
- OVER (上方穿过，如桥)
- ALONG (沿着)
- AROUND (绕过)

### 时间用介词
- AT (时间点) — at 8 o'clock
- ON (特定天) — on Monday
- IN (时间段) — in June
- BY (不晚于) — by Friday
- FOR (持续) — for 2 hours
- SINCE (起点) — since 2020
- UNTIL / TILL (终点) — until tomorrow
- DURING (期间) — during the meeting
- FROM...TO (起止) — from 9 to 5
- AGO (之前) — three days ago

### 易混对比组
- BY vs BESIDE vs NEXT TO — 都表示"旁边"，距离不同
- ABOVE vs OVER — above=正上方/高于，over=覆盖/跨过
- BELOW vs UNDER — below=低于，under=正下方/受控
- BETWEEN vs AMONG — between=两者之间，among=三者及以上之间
- IN vs INTO — in=在里面，into=进入
- ON vs ONTO — on=在上面，onto=上去
- THROUGH vs ACROSS — through=穿过内部，across=穿过表面
- AROUND vs ROUND — around=环绕/大约，round=圆形
- BY vs UNTIL — by=不晚于，until=一直到
- AS vs LIKE — as=作为/充当(身份)，like=像(比较)
